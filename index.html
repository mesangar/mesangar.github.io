Melani Sánchez García

I started my PhD. in Computer Vision in October 2017 at the Universidad de Zaragoza, where I am advised by José J. Guerrero and Rubén Martínez Cantín .

My main research interests lie in the simulated prosthetic vision. My work consists of investigating computer vision and deep learning techniques to be part of a prosthetic vision simulation system. This simulation system is based on blind people with retinitis pigmentosa disease. Through cameras we acquire information from the environment of interest that we later process in a computer simulating the prosthetic vision.
